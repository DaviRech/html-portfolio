About Me

Hi! My name is Davi Rech, and I’m passionate about continuous learning and personal growth. I’m currently preparing to return to BYU, where I aim to excel in my studies and become a reference in my field. Education has always been important to me, and I’m committed to achieving high grades and making the most of this opportunity.

Professionally, I’m focused on improving my skills to not only advance in my career but also to help my coworkers and ease their workload. I believe in being a supportive team member and making a positive impact wherever I go.

In my free time, I enjoy playing games like Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories, practicing English pronunciation, and helping friends like Néfi improve their language skills. I also have a great sense of humor, often joking about being the “favorite child” in my family—a title I proudly wear on custom T-shirts!

Whether in my personal life, studies, or work, I strive to be a positive influence, always learning, growing, and helping others along the way.
